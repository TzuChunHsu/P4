Getting driver's license

Now New Jersey can use Taiwan driver's license "without test" to directly change to US driver's license.

To renew a driver's license in New Jersey, the following conditions must be met:

  • Living in New Jersey: Bank Statement or Utility Bill showing New Jersey address
  • Possess SSN (Social Security Number Card) or can present relevant documents that cannot apply for SSN
  • Bring your Taiwan driver's license to Taipei Economic and Cultural Office in New York for driver's license translation

The translation of Taiwan driver's license is as follows:

appointment MVC

NJ MVC (NJ Motor Vehicle Commission)MVC
After entering, select Initial Permit to choose your favorite place to make an appointment At present, due to the epidemic situation, it is usually only possible to make an appointment two months later.

Before heading to MVC, make sure you bring:

  • passport
  • Original Taiwan driver's license + translation from the scripture office
  • Proof of address (Bank Statement or Utility Bill can choose one)
  • SSN card (Social Security Number and Card)
  • ATM cards issued by U.S. banks

MVC application process

  1. Queue to get the form (to get the number plate)
  2. Document confirmation (ID Check), confirm whether it meets the six-point certificate (obtain Permit)
  3. Document Review, confirm the six-point certificate again, issue a driver's license + take a photo (get a driver's license)
1. Queue to receive the form

Before entering the MVC, the guard at the gate will ask you to show the screenshot of the mobile phone you reserved, and you can enter after confirmation. After entering, you have to queue up to receive the form below (you must also show the screenshot of the appointment mobile phone when you get the form).

After receiving the number plate, follow the instructions to queue for the "ID Check"

2. ID Check

At this level, the undertaker will ask you to show the number plate just now, and ask you to provide:

  • passport
  • Original Taiwan driver's license + translation from the scripture office
  • Proof of address (Bank Statement or Utility Bill can choose one)
  • SSN card (Social Security Number and Card)
  • ATM card issued by a US bank
  • Form filled in the previous level
3. Review

After signing the documents, then paying $34 and taking a photo, you can get the temporary driver's license as shown below. At the same time, he will also inform that the real driver's license will be sent to the address we filled in the first pass form in three weeks.
