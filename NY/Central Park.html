Central Park

Central Park is the green heart of New York, a garden for New Yorkers, and a must-see when visiting New York.

The Bethesda Fountain is the center of the entire Central Park. Anyone who has watched the movie "Manhattan" should be familiar with it. There is a famous Angel of the Waters on the fountain, which commemorates the completion of New York's waterway system in the 19th century, so that New York finally has clean water to drink. Prior to this, New York had a severe cholera epidemic due to poor water quality. The angel held a lily in his left hand, a symbol of "purity".


Before walking to the Bethesda Fountain, you will first pass the Bethesda Platform. The magnificent and unique design here is the best place to watch the fountain. Because of its excellent location, you can overlook the entire square and the lakeside. It is recommended to take a complete picture of the fountain from here. The Bethesda platform is also the end of he Mall, where you can experience the romantic atmosphere of the movie scene together.

If you enter from the southeast entrance, you will pass the zoo in Central Park. There is a zoo in Central Park, but it is not large in scale. There is an additional fee for admission. The main feature is the sea lion show. When passing by, many people can see the sea lion show.


Next to the Zoo and Children's Zoo, there is a musical clock called Delacorte Clock. Every day from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, as long as it is on the hour or 30, there will be music playing, the animal band above will start dancing, and the music content will change with the seasons, the whole is super childlike.
